Press Conferences

One of the most anticipated events to take place at E3 are the press conferences that are held by multiple companies. Here, they show off trailers of new products and new games that are currently in development. Through the advancement of technology, this is where developers are able to show off their innovative projects to a large audience. This is also where gamers and fans can grow excited to purchase these products and play them in the future, and where developers can meet with publishers to talk about their products.

Show Floor

At the Show Floor, gamers can come together to try out some of the new games and products that were featured at E3 press conferences. This can lead to input from the fans that the developers can use to further develop their products. It is where gamers can get a small taste of whats to come for the games that they will be waiting several weeks or months for before they release.
